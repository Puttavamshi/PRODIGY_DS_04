## 📌 Internship

**Prodigy InfoTech – Data Science Internship**

---

## 🎯 Objective

To segment customers into meaningful groups based on their **annual income** and **spending behavior** using **K-Means Clustering**.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Scikit-learn

---

## 📊 Project Overview

This project focuses on **unsupervised learning** to identify hidden patterns in customer data.

Using:

* Annual Income (k$)
* Spending Score (1–100)

Customers are grouped into clusters to understand different types of buying behavior.

---

## ⚙️ Workflow

1. Data Loading (Seaborn dataset / custom dataset)
2. Feature Selection
3. Elbow Method to find optimal number of clusters
4. K-Means Clustering Model
5. Visualization of clusters
6. Identification of cluster centroids

---

### 🔹 Customer Segmentation Visualization

## 💡 Key Insights

* Customers are divided into **5 distinct segments**
* High income & high spending customers are the **most valuable group**
* Some high-income customers spend less → **marketing opportunity**
* Low income but high spending customers show **impulsive behavior**
* Clustering helps in **targeted marketing & business strategy**

## 🚀 Conclusion

K-Means clustering helps businesses:

* Understand customer behavior
* Improve targeting
* Increase revenue through personalization
